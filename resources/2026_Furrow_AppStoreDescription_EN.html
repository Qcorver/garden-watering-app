2. Full description

Stop guessing when to water — or prune — your garden. Furrow uses real evapotranspiration science (ET₀) combined with local rain history and weather forecasts to tell you exactly when your garden needs water, and reminds you when it's the right time to prune each of your plants.

Water smarter, not more. By watering only when your garden actually needs it, you reduce water waste, lower your water bill, and keep your plants thriving. Every drop counts — Furrow helps you make sure none are wasted.

Never miss the right pruning window again. Add your garden plants by name or photo, and Furrow tracks the optimal pruning months for each species. A push reminder arrives automatically when the season is approaching.

Unlike indoor plant care apps, Furrow is built specifically for outdoor gardens. No hardware, sensors, or smart hoses required. Just open the app and get a clear, personalised recommendation.

3. How it works

Furrow calculates daily water loss using the Hargreaves evapotranspiration method (ET₀) — the same approach used in professional agriculture. It factors in recent rainfall, temperature, your latitude, and the 5-day forecast to give you a personalised watering recommendation that adapts to your local climate.

For pruning, Furrow uses a curated plant database to determine the correct pruning months for each species. Add plants by searching the database or by taking a photo — Furrow identifies the species automatically. Plants due for pruning soonest appear at the top of your list, and a push notification arrives when the time is near.

4. What the app does

  • Best day to water — Scans the 5-day forecast and picks the start of the longest dry spell, so your soil stays moist as long as possible.
  • Per-category advice — Add your plants and get separate watering recommendations for herbs & vegetables, border plants & shrubs, drought-tolerant species, trees & native plants, and pots & containers. Each category has its own water needs.
  • How long to water — Shows how many minutes per m² to apply, based on your garden's actual water deficit.
  • Rainfall overview — Combines 7 days of measured rainfall with the 3-day forecast for a complete picture of your garden's water balance.
  • Watering calendar — Log the days you watered and track your garden's history month by month. Your logged watering sessions count toward your weekly water budget, keeping advice accurate.
  • Plant library & pruning calendar — Add your garden plants by searching our database or by taking a photo — Furrow identifies the species for you. Each plant shows its pruning window; plants due soonest appear at the top. Get a push reminder when it's time to prune.
  • Soil type setting — Choose your soil type (sandy, loamy, clay, chalky) so the advice accounts for how quickly your soil dries out.
  • Sensitivity slider — Adjust how often you receive a watering recommendation, from less frequent to more frequent, to match your own watering habits.
  • Push notifications — Receive a gentle morning reminder when watering is recommended. Notifications are only sent when your garden actually needs water.
  • GPS location — Use your current location or search for any city worldwide. The advice automatically adjusts for your latitude and climate.
  • Offline support — Last known weather data is cached locally, so the app stays useful even without an internet connection.

5. Designed for home gardeners

Whether you grow vegetables, flowers, or a lawn, Furrow helps you build a smarter watering habit — and remember when to prune. No complicated settings, no sensors. Just open the app, see the advice, and garden with confidence.
